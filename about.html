About Our Library

Welcome to the Library of the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Tiruchchirapalli . On floor 2 of the Sir C.V. Raman Academic Block, there is a library.
The goal of the library is to meet the academic and informational needs of all students, professors, and staff by providing information services, access to full-text digital and physical resources, and bibliographic databases. On all working days from 4:30 to 5:30 p.m., the library is open 358 days a year. Weekends and gazetted holidays are excluded from this schedule.
